краткое описание 

- [ cartographer ] Cartographer is a service providing an Asset and Listing inventory 
  for the purpose of providing metadata about financial instruments to Trading Infrastructure services.
  The service provides:
  - Ingestion and curation of metadata from 3rd party services
  - Delivery of metadata to downstream Trading Infrastructure services
  - API for services access to metadata
  - Frontend for user exploration and investigation
